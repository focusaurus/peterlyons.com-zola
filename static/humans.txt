Site by Peter Lyons. Visual Design by [Emphasis Creative](http://emphasiscreative.com).

# Technology Stack

* zola static site generator with tera HTTML templates and content in markdown
* SCSS for stylesheets
* Plus Party is coded in Elm

# Deployment

* Netlify for build and CDN hosting
